Priority: High

Title: Operational Risk
Summary: Operational risk is a broad category of risk that encompasses failures or disruptions related to an organization's day-to-day operations.
Risk Description:
A major airline experiences a sudden outage of its central reservation and check-in IT system, leading to the inability to process bookings, check passengers in, or issue boarding passes across multiple airports.
Causes:
  • Aging or insufficiently maintained IT infrastructure.
  • Lack of robust system redundancy or backup procedures.
  • Poor change management during software updates or system integrations.
  • Vendor failure, cyber-attack, or a cascade effect from power/network outages.
