How Notification Rules Work
Rule Types:
  • Mitigation Due Date: Notify about upcoming or overdue mitigation actions
  • Assessment Schedule: Notify about scheduled risk assessments
  • Risk Level Change: Notify when risk severity changes
  • Indicator Breach: Notify when KPI indicators breach thresholds
Conditions:
  • Is Overdue: Trigger when item is past due date
  • Due in X Days: Trigger X days before due date
  • Overdue by X Days: Trigger when X days overdue
  • Risk Level Filters: Only trigger for specific risk levels
Note: Rules are processed automatically by the system scheduler. Use the "Test Rules" button to manually trigger rule processing.
